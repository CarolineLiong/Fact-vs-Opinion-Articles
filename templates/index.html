FACTUAL VS NON-FACTUAL

Find out whether an article is fact-based or opinion-based



HOW IT WORKS?

We created this page to easily classify if a sentence, paragraph, or article is factual or non-factual (along with with the percentage of the classifier being accurate). This page is made using the Logistic Regression that had been trained on 99 older news articles with sentences classified as factual or nonfactual. Due to this training process, we recommend that you only enter sentences rather than whole articles for more accurate results.
